Breaking News

In June 2016, ArdaghGroup acquired 10 Ex-Ball Packaging Europe sites and 2 Ex-Rexam Beverage Can sites in Europe (along with 10 other sites in the US and Brazil). This acquisition completed on 30th of June 2016.

One of the sites acquired in Europe was also our Transactional Shared Service Center (SSC) in Serbia (Belgrade), which is the SSC for transactional finance and accounting support for all our 12 Europe sites in 9 countries (Austria, France, Germany, Netherlands, Poland, Serbia, Spain, Switzerland, UK). After 2 challenging years of providing F&A service to all of our business partners we built a strong corporate EU reputation resulting in standing proudly today with our respective counterparts face-to-face and shoulder-to-shoulder on highly competitive European Market.
Highlights of the Year End 2016:
  • AMB Serbia SSC Belgrade transferred additional activities (vendor debit balances and payment run approvals)
  • Identified only best practices across different locations in Europe and rolled these out in a standardized way to become fully applicable in our corporate environment
  • Optimized and harmonized all transactional F&A activities including improved cutting-edge automation of the processes in SAP
  • Prepared process-flow charts and process narratives improving understanding of the amended processes and controls to mitigate risks
  • All controls are documented in the RACM format satisfying audit requirements  (AMB Serbia has undergone 3 comprehensive audit circles being assessed highly positive with minimal and minor amendments for improvement in 2017)
To stay an Operationally Excellent BPO organization in today’s business, we must keep measuring our performance using performance scorecard but with all our focus on signed contractual commitments (Service Level Agreements).

Our SSC established a mechanism to measure our basic fundamental SSC engagement as it is our belief that we should know intimately the stats of each of our deals. Therefore, We approach all of our customers directly, we handle all the respected queries separated, and keep on identifying and selecting each SLA to make sure we satisfy both sides of the margin.

Our key scorecard is divided into 2 vital sections: (1) financial and (2) operational performance.
As our focus is always practical, if you want to hear more about our year end performance (2016) our staff will be more than willing to walk you through our business structure and all of our main achievements providing insight into latest core-visuals.
